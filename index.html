Welcome to the unofficial Beginners Mario Odyssey Speedrunning Tutorial!

Prerequesteries :

A Super Mario Odyssey Copy
A Nintendo Switch
A controller compatible with Nintendo Switch (That has motion controls)
A empty file slot
Smallant1's Movement Guide

Introduction :

Super Mario Odyssey is one of the greatest speedgames of all time. While it is easy to learn, it is difficult to master.
This site aims to give the easiest route for beating the game with decent speed, but it's far from the fastest.
